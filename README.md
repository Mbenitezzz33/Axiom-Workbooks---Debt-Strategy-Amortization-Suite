# Axiom Workbooks – Debt Strategy & Amortization Suite

## Overview

The Axiom Workbooks Debt Strategy & Amortization Suite is a collection of Excel-based financial modeling tools designed to help individuals better understand how debt works and how strategic repayment decisions can reduce both payoff time and total interest paid.

This bundle includes:

* Student & Personal Loan Payoff Strategy Workbook
* Home & Auto Loan Amortization Workbook

These tools combine financial modeling, dynamic dashboards, and interactive amortization schedules to simplify complex financial concepts and support better financial decision-making.

# Why These Workbooks Were Built

Most people make financial decisions based solely on the monthly payment.

These tools were built to answer questions such as:

* How much interest will I pay over the life of my loan?
* What happens if I pay an extra $50 or $100 each month?
* How much time can I save by making additional payments?
* Should I make a lump-sum payment?
* How quickly can I reduce debt and build equity?

The objective is to make amortization schedules and debt repayment strategies easier to understand through data, modeling, and visualization.
